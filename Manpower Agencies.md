### 🤖 AI-Powered Recruiting & Sourcing

### 👔 Specialized Recruiting Support
Provide services that require specific expertise.
*   **Contingent Staffing Support**: Many recruitment firms focus on direct hires. *   **Red Team Recruitment**: Test the quality of a recruitment firm's candidate pool by offering to source for their most difficult-to-fill roles. If you can find candidates they couldn't, it's a powerful proof of your capability.
*   **RPO (Recruitment Process Outsourcing)**: Act as an extension of a recruitment firm's own team during periods of high volume, taking over parts of their recruitment process to help them scale their delivery capacity.

### 📊 Back-Office & Consulting
Offer services to improve internal operations.
*   **Market Research & Benchmarking**: Provide specialized reports on topics like salary data, job benchmarking, and candidate availability. This helps recruitment firms add value to their clients and can be a paid add-on service.
*   **Onboarding & Compliance Management**: Offer a service to streamline the new hire onboarding process, ensuring all paperwork, contracts, and compliance checks are completed accurately and on time.

### 🛠️ Software, Platforms & Marketplaces
Develop or resell technology solutions.
*   **VMS/MSP Integration**: Recruiting firms often need to work with large clients who use Vendor Management Systems (VMS) like *Fieldglass* or *Beeline*. You can offer a service to help them navigate and win business within these managed programs.
*   **Agency Management Platforms**: Offer a software marketplace that allows recruitment firms to manage multiple talent vendors, source from a bench of pre-vetted candidates, and handle contracts and invoicing through a single platform, similar to *Sourcer* or *Avature CWM*.
